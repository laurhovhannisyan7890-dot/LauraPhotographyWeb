About Me

Hi, I’m Laura. I see every project as a conversation between people, places, and light. My aim is to help you feel comfortable and to shape your moments into timeless photos and films. Whether it’s a portrait, an event, or an everyday detail, I work with warmth, curiosity, and a cinematic sense of composition.

Experience Photography

Moments that tell a story – capturing the joy, connection, and atmosphere that make every experience unforgettable.

Architecture Photography

Gentleman’s Ride – The Hague, Netherlands (2024)

Classic & vintage style motorcycles riding for men’s health. A celebration of community and timeless style – where leather, chrome, and character meet a shared purpose. Captured on the streets of The Hague, combining energetic colour with a few black‑and‑white moments for a cinematic, nostalgic feel.

Videography

I also offer cinematic videography services — capturing movement, light, and atmosphere. Samples can be provided upon request.

Armenian Embassy – The Hague, Netherlands (2024)

Covered cultural and official ceremonies at the Armenian Embassy in the Netherlands, documenting diplomatic receptions, formal gatherings, and high-level networking events. The work reflects the respectful atmosphere, attention to detail, and cultural significance of each occasion.

Musician Portraits – The Hague, 2023

A commissioned portrait series for cellist Luka Stefanović, capturing the harmony between musician and instrument in an urban setting. Shot in natural light, the images blend the architectural textures of The Hague with the elegance and focus of a classical performer.
